The Best Movies According to Brendan

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Fired Up

While at its core, Fired Up is a raunchy teen comedy without a super memorable cast, this movie never fails to put a smile on my face.

Tick Tick Boom

I'm not a big fan of musicals, but Tick Tick Boom features an emotional performance from Andrew Garfield and left me feeling melancholic and hopeful despite the movie's sometimes somber tone.

The Batman

My favorite comic book movie to date, featuring a great take on Bruce Wayne by Robert Pattinson
